# Privacy Policy for Nitya

**Effective Date:** March 10, 2026

At **Nitya**, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the Nitya mobile application ("the App").

### 1. Information We Collect

#### A. Location Data
Nitya uses high-precision Vedic calculations to provide Panchang details specific to your current location (Sunrise, Sunset, Tithi, etc.). 
* **How it is used:** We request access to your device’s GPS location to calculate accurate astronomical timings for your specific coordinates.
* **Offline Processing:** Location data is processed locally on your device to perform offline calculations.
* **Storage:** We do not store your exact GPS coordinates on our servers.

#### B. Push Notifications
With your permission, the App sends alerts for recurring events such as Ekadashi, Purnima, and other Hindu festivals.
* **How it is used:** To provide the "Smart Alerts" feature you have opted into.
* **Management:** You can manage or disable these notifications at any time through your device settings.

#### C. Analytics
We use third-party analytics tools (such as Firebase Analytics or Apple App Analytics) to understand how the App is used.
* **What is collected:** This includes anonymous data such as device type, app crashes, and feature usage patterns.
* **Purpose:** This data is used solely to improve the user experience and performance of Nitya. It is not linked to your personal identity.

### 2. No Advertisements
Nitya provides a modern, clean experience. We do **not** display third-party advertisements within the App, and we do **not** share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

### 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We only share information when required to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect our or others' rights, property, or safety.

### 4. Third-Party Services
The App may contain links to our Support URL or other resources. Please note that these external sites have their own privacy policies, and we do not accept responsibility or liability for their content or activities.

### 5. Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. Since your core data (location) is processed locally on-device, the risk of data interception is significantly minimized.

### 6. Children’s Privacy
Our App does not specifically market to children under 13, nor do we knowingly collect personal information from them.

### 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
